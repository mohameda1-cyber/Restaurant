About

Abdu Ethiopian Cuisine offers delicious dining and takeout to Seattle, WA. Abdu Ethiopian Cuisine is a cornerstone in the Seattle community and has been recognized for its outstanding African cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff. Our African restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.
